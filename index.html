About

30+ Years at the
Edge of Computing

Clay Fandre has spent his career solving problems most computers can't. As a Senior HPC Engineer, he designs, deploys, and optimizes the high-performance computing infrastructure that powers breakthroughs in aerospace, defense, science, and enterprise — the clusters and supercomputing systems where ordinary hardware simply doesn't apply.

With a foundation in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Minnesota Duluth and over three decades in the field, Clay has built deep expertise across the full HPC lifecycle: from architecture and procurement through systems administration, performance tuning, automation, and multi-vendor consulting.

Currently at RedLine Performance Solutions — one of the most respected names in the HPC industry — Clay brings the same competitive precision to engineering that he once brought to the basketball court at UMD.
